# Day 01 – Ethical Hacking (Visual Understanding)

## Types of Hackers
```
Hacker
│
├── White Hat
│ ├── Works legally
│ ├── Has permission
│ └── Helps secure systems
│
├── Black Hat
│ ├── Works illegally
│ ├── No permission
│ └── Steals or damages data
│
└── Grey Hat
├── Between legal and illegal
└── May report issues without permission

```

## Ethical Hacking vs Illegal Hacking
```
Ethical Hacking
│
├── Permission is given
├── Goal is to find weaknesses
├── Helps fix security issues
└── Legal

Illegal Hacking
│
├── No permission
├── Goal is personal gain
├── Causes damage or theft
└── Illegal

```
